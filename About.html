What does the drumstick consist of?

Drum shells are made from birch, beech, maple, and oak, among other types of wood and materials.
They use two-ply and three-ply plywood.
From the left: oak, beech, birch, and maple plywood.
Plywood is made up of several thin layers of wood laminated together.
